To support the conference, you can choose one of the following sponsorship package or contact for dedicated options:

* **Bronze** (250.00€): Logo visibility on the website, in the digital proceedings, during the opening and closing ceremonies
* **Silver** (400.00€): Same as Bronze, plus the inclusion of a printed A4 flyer in the conference bag
* **Gold** (1000.00€): Same as Silver, plus a desk for three days outside one of the conference rooms, along with one registration and social dinner ticket
* **Platinum** (1500.00€): Same as Silver, plus the opportunity to give a one-hour presentation on the morning of the first day, before the official opening of the conference
